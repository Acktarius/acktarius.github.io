Swap wCCX on Polygon-Matic Network

this post is to help you to swap  wCCX on the Polygon/Matic network, this is not financial advice, you should do your own research for your investments
prerequisite:
_Metamask installed on your browser
_Sufficient coin to swap on Matic

Adding Matic network to Metamask



Select network and click on personalized RPC


Fill the informations for the Matic Network, and Save

you can find those here

Get the wCCX contract info

go on polygonscan and look for wCCX, copy the contract info

Swap on Sushi

go on Sushi swap and Select token


Select Tokens tab


Paste the contract information

Make sure your Metamask is on the Matic Network with enought MATIC to pay the transaction fee

The option to add wCCX token to your Metamask will be offered to you, you should add it, since for some reason it’s different from the one in the Matic wallet.

You’re all set, ready to swap !
